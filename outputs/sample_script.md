# Sample Script

## Product

Oversized Graphic T-Shirt

## 0-3s Hook

A model walks into frame wearing a clean oversized graphic T-shirt. The camera quickly pushes in to show the front print and relaxed fit.

## 3-8s Product Value

The model slightly turns, adjusts the hem naturally, and shows the fabric texture under soft daylight.

## 8-15s Conversion Scene

Cut to a lifestyle streetwear shot. The T-shirt is paired with casual pants and sneakers. End with a close-up of the print and fit.
